The Method
How It Works

Six steps. One system. Every subject.

Grounded in schema theory, retrieval practice, Bloom's Taxonomy and metacognitive research — the Velvet Method uses AI to make these principles accessible for every student. Tap a letter to see what it delivers.

VStep 1 · View
Map your whole syllabus before you study a thing, so nothing catches you out.
EStep 2 · Evaluate
Run an AI diagnostic to find exactly where your gaps are.
LStep 3 · Learn
Fill those gaps in the format that sticks — games, roleplay, the Feynman technique.
VStep 4 · Verify
Prove you know it with exam-style questions and instant examiner-style feedback.
EStep 5 · Explore
Push into top-grade thinking: application, analysis, unseen problems.
TStep 6 · Transform
Reflect, plan your next session, and lock it in with spaced repetition.

Why It Works

Built on real science.
Taught by real educators.

🧠

Schema Theory

The View step builds the mental framework before content is learned — new knowledge attaches more deeply to existing structure.

🔁

The Testing Effect

Retrieval practice in the Verify step is one of the most robust findings in cognitive science — far more effective than re-reading.

🎯

Bloom's Taxonomy

The Explore step targets higher-order thinking — application and analysis — which consistently differentiates grade boundaries.

🪞

Metacognitive Learning

John Hattie's research identifies self-regulation as one of the highest-impact educational interventions available.
