About

Built by an educator.

The AI Study Method was created by a physicist, teacher, and AI enthusiast who wanted to give every student access to the kind of personalised, structured learning that was previously only available to those who could afford a premium tutor.

The Story

Why the
Velvet Method exists.

After years of teaching — from university lecture halls in China to one-on-one tutoring sessions with individual students — a pattern became clear. The students who performed best weren't necessarily the most intelligent or the hardest working. They were the ones who studied strategically.

They knew what their syllabus required before they started. They identified their gaps early. They varied how they studied based on what worked for them. They tested themselves regularly rather than just re-reading. And they reflected on what they'd learned rather than simply moving on.

The problem was that nobody had ever taught most students these habits. They were the kind of meta-skills that were informally absorbed by some students — usually those with access to excellent tutors or highly educated parents — but never explicitly taught.

AI changes that equation completely. With the right framework, any student can now access the kind of personalised, adaptive, patient learning support that was previously out of reach. The Velvet Method is that framework.
