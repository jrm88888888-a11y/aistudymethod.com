Collision Theory — Temperature, Concentration, Surface Area & Catalysts

Only collisions with sufficient energy (≥ activation energy) and correct orientation cause a reaction. Increasing temperature has a disproportionate effect because it shifts the Maxwell–Boltzmann distribution, creating many more high-energy collisions.
