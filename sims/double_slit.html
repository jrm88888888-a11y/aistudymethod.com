Double-Slit Interference (Young's)

Coherent sources · fringe spacing w = λD/s · path difference · intensity distribution

Bright fringes appear where the path difference from the two slits is an integer number of wavelengths (constructive). Dark fringes appear at half-integer multiples (destructive). Fringe spacing w = λD/s.
