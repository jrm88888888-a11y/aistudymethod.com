Reversible Reactions & Equilibrium

Dynamic equilibrium and Le Chatelier's principle — N₂ + 3H₂ ⇌ 2NH₃

At dynamic equilibrium the forward and reverse reaction rates are equal and concentrations remain constant — but both reactions are still occurring. Le Chatelier's principle predicts which direction the system shifts to oppose a change.
