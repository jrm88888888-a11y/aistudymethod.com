Magnetic Fields & the Motor Effect

F = BIL · Fleming's left-hand rule · Field patterns around wires and solenoids

The force on a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field is perpendicular to both the current and the field direction. Doubling B, I, or L each doubles the force F = BIL.
