Equilibrium & Kc

Reversible reactions · ICE tables · Le Chatelier's principle · Q vs K

Kc is fixed by temperature alone. Adding or removing a species changes Qc, not Kc — the system shifts to restore equality. Dilution shifts equilibria involving unequal moles of gas towards the side with more moles.
