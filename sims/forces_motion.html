Forces & Motion

Newton's Second Law · Free-body diagrams · F = ma

A resultant (net) force causes acceleration. When forces are balanced the resultant is zero and the object moves at constant velocity — or stays still. F = ma links force (N), mass (kg), and acceleration (m/s²).
