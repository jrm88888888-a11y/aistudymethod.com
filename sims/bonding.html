Ionic & Covalent Bonding

Electron transfer (ionic) and electron sharing (covalent) — dot-and-cross diagrams

Ionic bonding involves transfer of electrons from metal to non-metal, forming oppositely charged ions that attract. Covalent bonding involves non-metals sharing pairs of electrons to fill their outer shells.
