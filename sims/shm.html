Simple Harmonic Motion

Mass–spring oscillator · displacement, velocity, acceleration and energy

In SHM the period T = 2π√(m/k) depends only on mass and spring constant — not on amplitude. Velocity leads displacement by 90°, acceleration opposes displacement by 180°.
