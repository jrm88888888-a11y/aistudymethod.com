The pH Scale

Acids · Alkalis · H⁺ and OH⁻ concentration · Indicators · Dilution

pH = −log₁₀[H⁺]. Each step on the pH scale is a 10× change in hydrogen ion concentration — pH 3 has 10,000× more H⁺ than pH 7.
