Capacitor Charge & Discharge

RC circuit · exponential curves · time constant τ = RC · 5τ rule

Voltage, charge, and current all decay (or rise) exponentially with the same time constant τ = RC. After 5τ the capacitor is within 1% of its final value — the "fully charged" rule of thumb.
