Enzyme Kinetics

Michaelis–Menten: v = Vmax[S] / (Km + [S])

Km is the substrate concentration giving half-maximal rate — a lower Km means the enzyme is more efficient at low substrate. Inhibitors shift Km or Vmax in characteristic, distinguishable ways.
