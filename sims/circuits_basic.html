Series & Parallel Circuits

Ohm's Law · V = IR · Resistance · Bulb brightness

In a series circuit, adding bulbs increases total resistance so each gets dimmer. In parallel, each bulb sees the full battery voltage — adding bulbs does not dim the others, but draws more current from the cell.
