Why switching wins — the probability tree

Initial pick is right 1/3 of the time and wrong 2/3. The host's knowledge is the lever — they always reveal a goat from the remaining two doors, which concentrates the 2/3 probability onto the single unopened door. Switching wins exactly when your original pick was wrong, which is 2/3 of the time.
