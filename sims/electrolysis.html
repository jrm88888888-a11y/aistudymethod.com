Electrolysis

Ion migration, electrode reactions, and the products formed

Reduction always occurs at the cathode (negative electrode) and oxidation at the anode. In aqueous solutions, the less reactive ion is discharged first — so concentrated brine gives Cl₂ at the anode, not O₂.
