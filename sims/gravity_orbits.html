Gravitational Fields & Orbits

g = GM/r² · vorb = √(GM/r) · T² ∝ r³ (Kepler's Third Law)

Increasing orbital radius slows a satellite — it takes longer to complete each orbit. Double the radius and the period grows by a factor of 2√2. The speed required to escape gravity entirely is √2 times orbital speed at the same radius.
